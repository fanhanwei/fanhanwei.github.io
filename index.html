FAN, Hanwei
樊瀚伟
I'm currently a PhD candidate in Reconfigurable computing system laboratory (RCSL) at the Department of Electronic & Computer Engineering in HKUST. I am honored to be supervised by Professor Wei ZHANG. Previously, I obtained my BEng Degree in Automation from Beijing Institute of Technology and my MSc Degree in Electronic Engineering from HKUST.

Research Interests

  • My current research focuses on DSE for Computer Architecture, EDA, Neural Network Compression, NAS, etc. I am interested in application of optimization/ML algorithms in DSE problems, especially experienced in solving DSE problems with BO and RL.
  • Key Words: DSE, Computer Architecture, EDA, Neural Network Compression, NAS, Optimization, ML, BO, RL.
